*Translation and Additional Observing Locations*

Astronomical observation sites are among the best places for space and astronomy enthusiasts to enjoy the beauty of the universe and learn the secrets of the sky. These sites provide an ideal environment for observing stars, planets, and other celestial bodies. They are typically located far from city light pollution, allowing for clearer and more detailed views of the night sky. In addition to Zarqa University, there are many other remarkable observation sites in Jordan, offering a unique experience for space lovers and enabling them to follow astronomical events such as eclipses, meteor showers, and more. Some notable sites include:

*Wadi Rum: Known for its dark skies and stunning desert landscape, it is a popular location for stargazing and observing meteor showers.
*Dana Biosphere Reserve: Offers clear skies and minimal light pollution, making it an excellent spot for astronomy activities.
*Al-Azraq Desert: Provides an open and dark environment perfect for observing celestial phenomena.
*Petra: Although primarily known for its archaeological significance, Petra also offers dark skies suitable for stargazing activities.
